Campaign Drop

Overview

For most campaign drop events:
The first tick occurs at UTC Friday at 00:02:48. (On my game's timer anyways.)
The ticks then happen every 5 minutes after that until UTC Thursday at 23:57:48.
Each tick, there is a 44% chance to drop the event currency.
If a drop occurs, there will be an evenly distributed chance of dropping 1, 2, or 3 of the event currency.

Why is this timer off from the in game timer?
You don't get drops during the last 24 hours of the event.
